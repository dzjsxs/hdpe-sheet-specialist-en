Product Introduction

Nylon Bushings and Sleeves are precision-machined bearing components that provide low-friction, self-lubricating support for rotating or sliding shafts. Manufactured from cast PA6 or PA66, these components offer an excellent alternative to bronze, brass, and sintered metal bearings in many applications, providing comparable performance at significantly lower cost and weight.

Nylon bushings are particularly suitable for light to medium load applications where corrosion resistance, chemical resistance, and maintenance-free operation are required. The material's ability to absorb shock and vibration reduces noise and protects mating metal components from wear. Oil-filled (MoS₂) grades provide enhanced tribological performance for demanding applications.
